My Counselling Approach

I have been trained as a Integrative Humanistic Counsellor.

My Counselling Approach

I believe that the relationship between you and myself is very important in enabling this process to progress. Alongside this, I also integrate TA to provide more structure and promote movement, moreover, I also use the three distinct types of ego states in personalities to allow you to identify patterns of relating. If appropriate or if we feel is required for our therapeutic relationship, I also integrate some of Gestalt techniques.
Gestalt focuses on completing unfinished business – I might bring these different approaches together in my practice work.
I am also specialised in addiction counselling, where I integrate this skills in the therapeutic process. If you participate in therapy, changes may start occurring which will help you feel more empowered and less worried. You may notice that you are increasing your self-esteem your inner peace and you are laughing more. You may feel excited about your life. I hope to meet you.
